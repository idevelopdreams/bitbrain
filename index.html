Brain+Bit Foundation seeks to foster the learning of new technologies to improve the quality of life in highly vulnerable areas in Latin America.


Our Pillars

Inclusion

Brain+bit Foundation helps people achieve quality training to work in the world of technology and the blockchain. We form young people between 14 and 25 years old, regardless of race, sex or nationality.

Education

Courses

Introduction to Blockchain
You will learn the basic concepts of Blockchain and become familiar with the technology.

Basic programming of public and private Blockchain
You will learn how to build a smart contract in a public and private decentralized Blockchain.

Specialized Program

In this program, you will learn how to program and create applicability of Blockchain technology.

Knowledge Groups

In Brain+Bit Foundation while you are in your learning process, you can be an active participant in our knowledge management groups, in which you will share your knowledge with other students, with tutors and people in the sector.
