Our Mission

The Brain+Bit Foundation seeks to foster the learning and adoption of new technologies to improve the quality of life in highly vulnerable areas in Latin America, establishing itself as the main benchmark in the formation of a new generation of Professionals.

Our Pillars

Education

Introduction to Blockchain: You will learn the basic concepts of Blockchain and become familiar with the technology.
Basic programming of public and private Blockchain: You will learn how to build a smart contract in a public and private decentralized Blockchain.
Specialized Program: In this program, you will learn how to program and create applicability of Blockchain technology.

Inclusion

Brain+bit Foundation helps people achieve quality training to work in the world of technology and the blockchain. We form young people between 14 and 25 years old, regardless of race, sex or nationality.

Knowledge Groups

In Brain+Bit Foundation while you are in your learning process, you can be an active participant in our knowledge management groups, in which you will share your knowledge with other students, with tutors and people in the sector.
